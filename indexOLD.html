About

I'm Eriq Lucero, a systems engineer passionate about solving complex challenges in aerospace, defense, and manufacturing. My journey has taken me from optimizing avionics and MBSE processes at Boeing to enhancing autonomous rail inspection systems and streamlining manufacturing efficiency. With a strong foundation in mechanical, electrical, and software engineering, I thrive in high-stakes environments where precision and innovation are key. My experience spans mission-critical projects like the USAF E-7A Rapid Prototype and Ground-Based Midcourse Defense programs, where I’ve led teams, improved processes, and ensured system validation. Alongside my technical work, I’m currently pursuing an MBA at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, refining my leadership and business strategy skills to drive meaningful impact. Beyond engineering, I’m passionate about mentorship, leadership development, and career growth. I know firsthand how challenging it can be to navigate the job market and land the right opportunities. That’s why I’m dedicated to helping college students and fellow engineers build strong résumés, sharpen their skills, and secure their dream jobs. Whether through guidance, mentorship, or sharing insights from my own journey, I want to empower the next generation of professionals to succeed. Let’s build the future together—one career at a time.
